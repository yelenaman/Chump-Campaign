How to Play

This game gives you the opportunity to play against (and lose to) the Great Ronald Chump and other members of his family. This week, the family members are Chumpina, Ronald's 49th wife, and little Chumpy the baby. One thing you might notice is that this game has no green cards, as Ronald Q. Chump does not believe in them.

The goal of the game is to use up all of your cards before anyone else. To accomplish this, one plays a card of similar color or number on top of the previously played card. There are special cards like those that make you draw 2 and 4 additional cards, wild cards that change the current color based on the player's choice, and reverse/skip cards, that skip the next player's turns or reverse the direction of play, respectively.

Before you play your second-to-last card, be sure to call uno by pressing the "call UNO" button. If you fail to call uno, you will recieve a penalty of two cards.

Have fun!
