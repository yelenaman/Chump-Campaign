How to Play

The goal of the game is to use up all of your cards before anyone else. To accomplish this, one plays a card of similar color or number on top of the previously played card. There are special cards like those that make you draw 2 and 4 additional cards, wild cards that change the current color based on the player's choice, and reverse/skip cards, that skip the next player's turns.

Have fun!
